The Gap
📧
Email / CRM — completely vacant
Brit's role has been empty since she left. CircleDNA is a one-time purchase with a long consideration window — email is the most cost-efficient channel to nurture leads from awareness to conversion, keep the brand top of mind, and drive referrals post-purchase. Without it, every unconverted paid lead is simply lost.
📉 What happened in June
The last email sent to CircleDNA's list was May 31st. June revenue dropped -29% vs May. We can't prove causation — but with no other channel actively nurturing leads through the consideration window, email going dark is the most likely contributing factor. This is the clearest real-world signal we have for the CRM hire.
